About

Engineering with structure, evidence, and long-term thinking.

I am interested in how software engineering, chemical and process engineering, and design engineering can inform one another. This blog exists as a place to document that overlap: lessons from systems design, engineering practice, process thinking, and the refinement of technical judgment.

Good engineering should feel composed. That principle shapes both the work and the writing here: clear structure, readable decisions, careful tradeoffs, and enough depth to be genuinely useful.
